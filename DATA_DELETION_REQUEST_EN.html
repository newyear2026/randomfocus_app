Data Deletion Information

RandomFocus takes your privacy very seriously. This page provides information on how to delete your data.

Types of Data Collected

1. Data Stored in Local Device Storage

  • Timer History: Focus session records (start time, end time, session status, etc.)
  • Spin Usage Records: Daily spin usage count and date information
  • Language Settings: Selected app language preference
  • App Settings: In-app preferences and settings
Important: All this data is stored only in your device's local storage and is not transmitted to any server.

2. Advertising-Related Data

  • Advertising ID: Advertising identifier collected through Google Mobile Ads
Note: Advertising IDs are shared with Google Mobile Ads and used to provide personalized advertisements.

How to Delete Your Data

Since all user data is stored only in local device storage, uninstalling the app will automatically delete all data.

Method 1: Uninstall the App (Simplest Method)

1 On Android devices:
  • Go to Settings > Apps > RandomFocus
  • Tap "Uninstall" or "Remove"
2 Confirmation:

When you uninstall the app, the following data will be deleted:

  • All timer history
  • Spin usage records
  • Language settings
  • All app settings
⚠️ Warning: Uninstalling the app will permanently delete all stored data. Please consider backing up any important records before uninstalling.

Method 2: Reset Advertising ID (Advertising Data Only)

To reset only your advertising ID, follow these steps:

1 On Android devices:
  • Go to Settings > Google > Ads
  • Select "Reset advertising ID"
2 Or disable ad personalization:
  • Go to Settings > Google > Ads
  • Disable "Ad personalization" option

Data Retention Period

Local Device Storage Data

  • Retention Period: While the app is installed
  • Automatic Deletion: Immediately upon app uninstallation
  • Additional Retention Period: None (data is completely deleted immediately upon app uninstallation)

Advertising ID

  • Retention Period: According to Google's Privacy Policy
  • Deletion Method: Reset advertising ID in device settings
  • Reference: Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Data Deletion Confirmation

To confirm data deletion:

  1. Uninstall the app
  2. Reinstall the app
  3. Verify that all previous data has been deleted
Verification Checklist:
  • Timer history is empty
  • Spin usage records are reset
  • Language settings return to default
